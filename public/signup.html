Privacy Policy

Effective Date:01/04/2025

GuardPoint ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you use our cyber security training platform.

1. Who We Are

GuardPoint is a cyber security training platform designed for employees with no prior IT skills. The platform is operated by an individual developer.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you create an account:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Email address
  • Hashed password (we never store plain-text passwords)
We do not use cookies or tracking technologies, and we do not collect any sensitive or financial information.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide personalised training emails based on your activity
  • To generate feedback for company administrators about their employees’ progress
  • To manage and secure user accounts
We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

4. How We Store Your Data

All user data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas, a cloud-hosted database with built-in encryption and access controls. Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing algorithms.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If your account becomes inactive for more than 12 months, your data may be permanently deleted after a review.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your account information. You can:

  • View and update your profile from the home page
  • Deletion of your account and personal data at any time from the home page

7. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Hashed passwords
  • Limited database access controls

8. Children's Privacy

GuardPoint is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. You will be notified of any significant changes via the platform or email.
