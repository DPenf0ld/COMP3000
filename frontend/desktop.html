Create a secure Password Check

Create a strong password to keep your accounts safe from hackers. Strong passwords protect your personal and sensitive information from being stolen. Weak passwords are easy for cybercriminals to crack, so following these tips can help you keep your data secure and reduce the risk of your accounts being hacked. A strong password isn't just a good idea‚Äîit's a necessity in today's world.

Password Strength Tips

  • Length: Use at least 8 characters (12 or more is even better).
  • Lowercase Letters: Include at least one lowercase letter (e.g., a, b, c).
  • Uppercase Letters: Include at least one uppercase letter (e.g., A, B, C).
  • Numbers: Add at least one number (e.g., 1, 2, 3).
  • Special Characters: Use at least one special character (e.g., !, @, #, $).

Important: Now that you've created your password, it's time to test it against a real-time breach database to see if it's been compromised in any data breaches. Don't worry‚Äîyour password is not shared or saved during this process.

You can also test any of your current passwords to check for breaches.
